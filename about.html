ABOUT US

Our Story

Mukuru Community Justice Center, is a community based organization that was formed in the year 2018, with the aim of
addressing cases of social injustices in our community. The organization is youth led and located in Mukuru Kwa Njenga Slum.

Mukuru Community Justice Center is part of the growing Social Justice Center Working Group.

It has 20 members who are passionate about defending human rights violation.

Our offices are located opposite Embakasi Girls Secondary.

Our Vision

To have a just community, with zero human rights violation.

Our Mission

To totally address the issues of social injustice and
see a community that respects human rights.

Our Objectives

  1. To speak against any type of human rights violation.
  2. To give legal advice.
  3. To refer any victim whose rights have been violated to the right authorities.
  4. To create an environment that has zero human rights violation.
